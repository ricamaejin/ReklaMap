Register

In order to ensure that we can properly address your concerns, you are required to register your information before submitting a complaint. This allows us to verify your identity and follow up with you regarding the outcome of your submission.

Select Category

HOA Member

An individual who is officially registered with the Homeowners Association (HOA) and has all the rights and responsibilities that come with membership.

Family of a HOA Member

Someone who is not an HOA member themselves but has a close family member—such as a parent, spouse, or sibling—who is a registered HOA member.

Non-HOA Member

A person who is not part of the HOA and does not have any immediate family members who are members of the HOA.
